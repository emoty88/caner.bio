GVG GeoMarketing

As part of the GVG GeoMarketing project, I played a pivotal role in the transformation of the system into a powerful geospatial planning and marketing tool. Initially developed as an MVP, the system faced scalability challenges when Coca-Cola Turkey started utilizing it and importing millions of data points. To address this, I took charge of the project and led the efforts to restructure the system. I implemented a microservice architecture and migrated the database from a relational database to MongoDB. Leveraging the capabilities of Elasticsearch, the system became capable of efficiently handling and analyzing large volumes of data.

As a result of these efforts, the company shifted its focus from mid-small businesses to market-leading companies. The enhanced system empowered clients to leverage advanced location-based marketing insights and planning capabilities, effectively reaching their target audiences and making data-driven decisions.
